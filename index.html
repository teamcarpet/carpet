CARPET REWARDS // TEAM BUYBACK PROGRAM
Team Buyback
When a token hits volume milestones, the Carpet protocol uses reserve SOL to buy back and burn tokens from the open market. Supply goes down, price support goes up. Rewards flow to everyone holding — not just devs.
01
Hit Volume
Your token crosses a 24h volume milestone (T1–T5). Measured live on-chain, no gaming possible.
02
Team Buys Back
Carpet reserve wallet auto-purchases tokens from Raydium. Size of buyback scales with the tier you hit.
03
Burn Forever
Bought tokens sent to a dead address. Circulating supply shrinks. Every remaining holder gets a bigger slice.
